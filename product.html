Product

Solution, features & differentiation

Eco-Eye is a behavior-change platform delivered as an AI camera and companion app — designed to make sustainability visible, actionable, and engaging for every household member.

Our Solution

An AI Eye That Watches
Over Your Home

Eco-Eye is a smart camera system that understands your home environment, identifies wasteful behaviors as they occur, and guides your household toward more sustainable choices — one action at a time.

Unlike passive energy monitors that only report consumption after the fact, Eco-Eye acts in real time. Its AI engine observes household activity, identifies specific waste events — a light left on in an unoccupied room, a running tap, an appliance left idle — and immediately delivers a personalized recommendation through the companion app.

By displaying the CO₂ saved and energy conserved from each corrected behavior, Eco-Eye creates a tangible, motivating link between daily action and real-world environmental impact. Over time, this feedback loop drives lasting behavioral change at the household level.

Product Features

Built to Change Behavior,
Not Just Track It

Eco-Eye 2.0 ships with four core features that work together to deliver a complete behavior change platform for the modern household. Each feature is designed with long-term engagement in mind.

Core AI Engine

Waste Detection AI

The camera's computer vision engine identifies electricity waste, water waste, and over-running appliances in real time — requiring zero manual logging from the user. The system learns each home's usage patterns and improves accuracy over time.

Zero manual input required
Intelligent Guidance

Smart Suggestions

When waste is detected, Eco-Eye delivers a timely, personalized recommendation through the companion app. Suggestions are calibrated to each user's individual behavior patterns, making them relevant, actionable, and welcomed rather than intrusive.

Personalized per household member
Gamification Layer

Eco Score System

Every sustainable action earns points in a daily "green" scoring system. Households build streaks, unlock achievement levels, and track their ranking — creating sustained motivation that far outlasts any one-time awareness campaign.

Drives long-term engagement
Household Engagement

Family Challenge Mode

The entire household participates together. Family Challenge Mode introduces friendly competition between members, making sustainability a shared household goal rather than an individual obligation — and significantly strengthening product retention.

Increases household retention

Why Eco-Eye

Three Reasons Eco-Eye
Stands Apart

Generic smart home devices monitor energy use. Eco-Eye changes the way people relate to energy use. That distinction is our defensible competitive advantage.

01

A Truly Integrated Platform

Eco-Eye combines hardware (AI camera), software (companion app), and behavioral science (gamification) into a single, seamless experience. Competing approaches are fragmented — requiring users to piece together separate monitors, apps, and dashboards. Eco-Eye delivers the complete platform out of the box.

02

Context-Aware AI Intelligence

Eco-Eye is not merely an AI product — it is an intelligent AI agent that understands its surroundings. It does not simply measure kilowatts or detect heat. It recognizes specific behaviors in specific contexts: a television running in an empty room, a tap left on after handwashing. This contextual intelligence underpins the entire behavior-change capability.

03

We Change Behavior, Not Just Data

Passive monitoring tools tell you what happened. Eco-Eye tells you what to do differently — and makes you want to do it. Through eco scores, smart suggestions, and family challenges, Eco-Eye drives measurable, durable shifts in household behavior. That is the only outcome that produces real carbon reduction at scale.
