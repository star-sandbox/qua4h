About Eco-Eye

We are building the intelligence layer for the sustainable home — starting in Vietnam, with a product designed for real behavior change.

Company Overview

Redefining Sustainability
at the Household Level

Eco-Eye is a Vietnam-based climate-tech startup building the first AI-powered carbon footprint camera designed for the modern home. Our mission is to make sustainable living visible, measurable, and genuinely rewarding.

We believe that meaningful climate action begins not in boardrooms — it begins in kitchens, living rooms, and hallways. Eco-Eye transforms every household into an active participant in carbon reduction by detecting waste, building awareness, and motivating lasting change through intelligent, context-aware technology.
