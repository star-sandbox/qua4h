Roadmap & expansion

From R&D to commercial launch and nationwide coverage — how Eco-Eye evolves by generation and by city.

Product Roadmap

A Clear Path Forward

Eco-Eye is designed to evolve. Each product generation delivers greater intelligence, autonomy, and integration — building toward a fully capable home sustainability platform.

2026
Current Phase

Research & Development

Core product development: AI model training, camera hardware selection, companion app architecture, and user experience design. Building the technical and business foundation for a 2027 market launch.

AI Model Training Hardware Prototyping App Development Market Research
2027–2028
Eco-Eye 2.0

Commercial Launch & Expansion

Commercial launch in Ho Chi Minh City in 2027, expanding to Hanoi in 2028. Eco-Eye 2.0 ships with the full core feature set, delivering a complete behavior change experience to early adopters in Vietnam's two largest urban markets.

Waste Detection AI Smart Suggestions Eco Score System Family Challenge Mode
2029
Eco-Eye 3.0

Next Generation & Nationwide

Nationwide expansion across Vietnam alongside the launch of Eco-Eye 3.0 — a smarter, smaller, more capable device featuring solar-powered operation, AI sleep mode for self-optimized energy use, and deep integration with smart home ecosystems.

Solar Power AI Sleep Mode Smart Home Integration Nationwide Coverage
Expansion Plan

Building Market by Market

Eco-Eye's expansion strategy is built for depth before breadth — establishing strong market penetration in each city before moving to the next, while continuously deepening existing markets year over year.

2026

Foundation

Product development, team formation, and go-to-market preparation. Partnership outreach and early-adopter community development.

2027

Ho Chi Minh City Launch

Commercial launch in Vietnam's largest urban market. Initial rollout through e-commerce with targeted community campaigns. Establish first-mover brand presence.

2028

Expansion to Hanoi

Northern market entry while deepening Ho Chi Minh City penetration. Electronics retail channel activation. Cross-city community challenge campaigns.

2029

Nationwide & Eco-Eye 3.0

Nationwide distribution across Vietnam as Eco-Eye 3.0 launches. Smart home partnership channel activation. Consolidation as Vietnam's leading household sustainability platform.
